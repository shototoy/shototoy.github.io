What is Salpakan?

Salpakan is an ancient Filipino strategy game brought to life for the modern era. Combining traditional gameplay with cutting-edge technology, it's a game of tactical brilliance and calculated risks.

Each piece holds unique powers. Position them wisely, anticipate your opponent's moves, and emerge victorious. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive strategist, Salpakan offers endless depth and excitement.

How to Play

Objective

Eliminate or capture your opponent's Flag, or successfully maneuver your own Flag to the opposite end of the board to claim victory.

Setup

Player 1 deploys pieces on the bottom 3 rows (rows 5, 6, 7).

Player 2 deploys pieces on the top 3 rows (rows 0, 1, 2).

Arrange your 21 pieces strategically. Your opponent cannot see your piece ranks until battle!

Movement

Move one piece per turn to an adjacent square: forward, backward, or sideward.

No diagonal moves allowed. Players alternate turns.

Combat Rules

Higher ranks defeat lower ranks.

Equal ranks eliminate each other.

Special: Spy defeats all officers (5-Star to Sergeant), but only Private defeats Spy.

Any piece can capture the Flag.

Strategy Tips

Protect Your Flag: Hide it well in your deployment. A captured Flag means instant defeat!

Use Your Spies: They eliminate high-ranking officers but are vulnerable to Privates. Deploy wisely!

Scout Carefully: Sacrifice lower-ranked pieces to identify enemy positions before committing your generals.

Control the Center: Dominate the middle of the board for maximum mobility and strategic options.
